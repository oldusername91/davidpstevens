Photos

General Info

Perhaps the most famous of the Illawarra's Lookouts. The Bald Hill site gives sweeping views of the escarpment and coast from Stanwell Park to Wollongong.

Map

Getting There

The Bald Hill Lookout is located along Otford Rd, just 200m from the intersection between Sir Lawrence Hargrave Drive and Otford Rd
