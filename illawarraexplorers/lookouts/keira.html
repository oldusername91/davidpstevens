General Info

Mt Keira Lookout is located at the peak of Mt Keira. The lookout is a popular picnic, hiking and abseiling site. Access to several other tracks are located from the park area.

Getting There

Access is from along Queen Elizabeth Dr and is very well signposted.

At the peak of Mt Ousley Rd, you can access Queen Elizabeth Dr from Picton Rd.The road is completely sealed and you should have no problems getting there in any vehicle

Utilities

Toilets and plenty of carparking are available.
