Photos

General Info

Jameberoo Mountain Lookout is located along Jamberoo Mountain Road once the road finishes ascending the mountain.

The view from the lookout is spectecular and the panorama stretches north from Port Kembla to Kiama in the South.

Map

Getting There

Jamberoo Mountain Road can be accessed via the Illawarra Highway east of Robertson (just near Robertson pie shop) or from Jamberoo Road just North of the village (Caution Jamberoo Mountain Rd is steep grade, contains hair pins and is very narrow sometimes).
