General Info

Robertsons Knoll Lookout is located along a 100 metre trail from a carpark on Harry Graham Drive. It is secluded and in most cases visits to the lookout you will have it all to yourself.

Map

Getting There

Robertsons Knoll Lookout is located along Harry Graham Drive. You can access Harry Graham Drive from either Mt Keira Rd from the west, or from Mt Ousley Rd from the South
