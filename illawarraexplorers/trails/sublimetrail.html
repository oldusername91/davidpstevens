Sublime Point Trail

QUICK INFO

  • 24 Hour Access
  • Scenic Site
  • Hike Grade: HARD

Photos

General Info

This track ascends the steep Illawarra Escarpment from the foothills in Austinmer to the Sublime Point Lookout. This track is for very fit walkers only

Getting There

Access to the trail is located from the beach side suburb of Austinmer. Parking is located at the cul-de-sac on Foothills Rd.
