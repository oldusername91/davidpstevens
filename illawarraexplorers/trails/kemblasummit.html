Mt Kembla Summit Trail

  • 24 Hour Access

Photos

General Info

The Mount Kembla Summit Track is a 2.4 km return trip from the carpark at Cordeaux Rd five minutes north of Mt Kembla. The trail is marked by a wooden sign and information board. Walkers are cautioned that the trail may be loose in places and to ensure any children are supervised at all times. At the summit again walkers are cautioned as the lookout does not have a fence at the cliff face. The view is spectacular and the surrounding area is quite suitable for a picnic.

Getting There

Access by car is from Unanderra. Accessing Cordeaux Rd and following it past Mt Kembla Village and Windy Gully.
