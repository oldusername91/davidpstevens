Sublime to Bulli Trail

QUICK INFO

  • 24 Hours
  • Scenic Site
  • Grade: Regular

Photos

General Info

This path connects the Bulli Lookout to the Sublime Point Lookout. Less than 2.5 km apart the trail passes through bushland and at times spectacular views can be seen from the trail.

Getting There

This track is located at the southern end of the Sublime Point picnic area. Plenty of parking is available.
