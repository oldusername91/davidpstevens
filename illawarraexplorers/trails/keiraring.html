Mt Keira Ring Trail

QUICK INFO

  • 24 Hour Access
  • Scenic Site
  • Hike Grade: Regular

Photos

General Info

Mt Keira Ring Trail is an 8.6km trail that circles Mt Keira. There are several entry points to the trail littered across the base of the mountain. Depending on what route is taken it is an easy grade walk through the finest of the Illawarra Escarpment's rainforest.

Getting There

The suggested entry point is located at the Byarong Park along Mt Keira Rd.
