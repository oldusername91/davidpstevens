General Info

The Ken Ausburn Track is a steep grade ascent up the eastern face of Mt Keira. The entrance is located near the Robson's Rd entrance to the University of Wollongong's Keiraville Campus. An art installation has been installed to honor Ken Ausburn, who was a former academic at the University who frequented the track. The track also has several original installments of the Mt Keira coal mine that are marked along the trail.

Getting There

Parking along Robsons Rd is ample on the weekend however on weekdays parking can be hard to come by. The entrance to the path is located opposited to the roundabout at Robsons Rd and Northfields Ave.
