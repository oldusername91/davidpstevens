Macquarie Pass Lower Trail

QUICK INFO

  • 24 Hours
  • Scenic Site
  • Grade: Hard

Photos

General Info

One of the several trails that extend from Macquarie Pass. This particular walk takes you along the Minnamurra Rivulet to pristine water holes perfect for swimming. The trail itself is quite rugged and only for confident hikers.

Getting There

At the base of Macquarie Pass on the Illawarra Highway there is a carpark on the south side of the road. Entrance to the trail is marked.
